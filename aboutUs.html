About Us: Raise to Rise


The meaning of Raise and Rise?


The phrase "Raise to Rise" beautifully captures the charity's core purpose and operational cycle. It functions both as a call to action and a statement of outcome. The first part, "Raise," refers to the active steps taken by the community: raising funds, resources, and institutional support through the generous donations from kind-hearted individuals and organizations like Eidi Centre and JDC. This collective effort is the engine that provides the necessary resources to fuel the students' journey. The second part, "to Rise," signifies the ultimate goal for the underprivileged students. It means enabling them to rise above their challenging socio-economic circumstances, rise to meet their full academic potential, and ultimately rise to success in their chosen careers and future lives. In essence, the motto distills the organization's philosophy into a single, compelling idea:

"We raise up the resources so that they may rise up in life."

Success Stories – RTR

Real students. Real struggles. Real success.

This page highlights the journeys of students who overcame challenges with the support of RTR. Each story showcases determination, resilience, and the impact of guidance and mentorship.

Ayesha

Became a Medical Student

Ayesha struggled with tuition fees and exam preparation. RTR helped with scholarship guidance. Today she is proudly studying MBBS.

Hamza

Secured a Full Scholarship Abroad

Hamza dreamt of studying overseas and faced paperwork and application hurdles. RTR volunteers guided him through the process and helped him win a full scholarship.

Fatima

Topper in Her Board Exams

Fatima faced financial challenges and lacked proper study resources. RTR supported her with study plans and academic counseling. She scored among the highest in her board exams.

Bilal

Accepted into an Engineering Program

Bilal was confused about career choices. RTR mentors guided him, helping him discover his passion for engineering. He is now enrolled in a top engineering university.

Hiba

Won a Laptop Through RTR Aid

Hiba couldn’t afford a laptop needed for her IT course. Thanks to RTR donors, she received one and is now thriving in her programming classes.

Sara

Excelled in STEM Competitions

Sara faced limited resources for STEM projects. RTR provided mentorship and materials. She now has multiple awards in science competitions and is pursuing engineering.

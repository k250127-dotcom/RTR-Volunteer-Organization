ABOUT US

Raise to Rise (RTR) was founded by a dedicated group of volunteers who recognized a significant challenge: talented students often miss out on higher education simply because the path to financial aid is complex and confusing. We saw a need for accessible, reliable support, and we stepped up to fill it.
